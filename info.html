Making A Purchase


Making a purchase couldn't be easier. Just browse our store, and add any items that you wish to buy into the shopping basket. After you have finished your selection, click on 'checkout' and you will be asked for a few details that we need to be able to complete the order.

We accept credit card payments. If you are shopping from outside of the UK your credit card company will convert the transaction into your own currency.

We accept Visa, Visa Debit, Mastercard, Solo, Maestro and Visa Electron. Back ordered items will be charged at the time of placing the order and will be shipped as soon as they become available. You may send your credit card information via phone, fax, mail or over the internet.

When confirmation of your order is received, this is to indicate that we have received your order. It does not indicate that a contract exists between us. We will indicate acceptance of your order, and hence a contract between us, when we send you an invoice. We have included this term to protect us in the case that a mistake has been made in pricing, we have inadvertently under-priced goods, or we are no longer able to supply a particular product for some reason. In the case of a change of price, we will always contact you first to ensure that the price is acceptable.


Cancelling an order

RSPCA strives to process your order immediately after checkout so you can receive your goods as soon as possible. Once you have hit the purchase button at checkout we begin to process your order.

If you decide after checkout that you would like to cancel your order please call 0333 240 5944 or email rspca@cbfulfilment.co.uk If you call or email within 24 hours your credit card will be credited for the entire purchase price and the cost of standard postage as long as you refuse delivery and "Return to Sender" when the order arrives. If you do not call within 24 hours to cancel, unfortunately we will not be able to refund your postage charges.

How long will my order take to arrive?

Your order will be processed upon receipt and should be delivered within 7 working days if items are in stock (addresses outside of the UK mainland will take longer). Please allow enough time for any onward postage of cards and gifts and order early to avoid disappointment.

Larger sized parcels are sent via couriers DPD and Parcel Force and if a mobile number is provided you will be advised via text message of the anticipated delivery date. Smaller sized parcels will be delivered by the Royal Mail.

We are currently experiencing an extremely high number of orders and calls and are therefore experiencing some delays for which we apologise.

Please be aware that your order or your refund may take longer than usual to arrive, and certain lines may not always be in stock due to various supply delays. Thank you in advance for your understanding.


Do you offer next day delivery?

RSPCA are delighted to offer an express delivery service. Due to the current pandemic we are unable to offer express/next day delivery at this time. We are sorry for the inconvenience caused and we thank you for your continued support during this time.

The cost of express delivery is £8.95 for any size of order and can be selected during checkout.
This service is only available for UK mainland addresses, excluding Scottish Highlands.

Please place your order before 12 noon from Monday to Thursday and we will despatch your parcel promptly for next day delivery.

Returns Policy


- We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it to us within 30 days of purchase for a prompt refund.


Returning an item

For a refund, items must be returned within 30 days of purchase with the original packaging.

It is acceptable to return an item with shrink-wrap removed, as long as the item has not been used or damaged after receipt.

You will receive a full refund, including the cost of delivering the goods to you.
